# AdvisorFlow AI

A forward-deployed AI prototype for client meeting preparation.

## Problem

Advisors, account managers, and customer success teams spend 30-60 minutes manually
preparing for client meetings: reviewing CRM records, meeting notes, open tasks, and
internal playbooks. Prep quality varies by person, and important risks get missed.

## What this does

The advisor selects a client. AdvisorFlow AI retrieves relevant context (CRM data,
meeting notes, tasks, account activity, internal playbooks) and generates a
source-grounded meeting prep brief, with a human reviewer in the loop.
