# Product Brief

Problem:
Advisors spend too much time manually gathering context before client meetings.
Prep quality varies, and important follow-ups can be missed.

User:
Financial advisor, customer success manager, or account owner.

Prototype goal:
Generate a source-grounded meeting prep brief from client data, notes, tasks,
account activity, and internal playbooks.
Success criteria:

- Reduces manual prep effort
- Cites relevant sources
- Flags missing or risky information
- Produces useful talking points and next steps
- Keeps the human reviewer in control
