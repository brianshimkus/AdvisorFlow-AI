# Playbook: Onboarding Checklist

Use when a client is in the "Onboarding" relationship stage.

- Confirm the kickoff call happened and data migration status before the
  meeting — do not assume it is complete.
- Ask about compliance or security requirements early (e.g. HIPAA, SOC 2)
  rather than discovering them mid-implementation.
- Set expectations that the first 30 days are about proving core value, not
  covering every feature.
- If account activity or notes are sparse, say so explicitly rather than
  filling gaps with assumptions — new accounts often have thin history.
