# Playbook: Renewal Conversations

Use when a client is within 90 days of contract expiration.

- Confirm the champion relationship is still intact. If the original
  champion has left or changed roles, prioritize identifying the new
  economic buyer before discussing pricing.
- Tie the renewal conversation back to the original business goals on file,
  and show progress (or lack of it) against them.
- If usage has declined, name it directly and ask why rather than waiting
  for the client to bring it up.
- Loop in an account manager before mentioning any discount — do not
  improvise pricing concessions.
