# Playbook: Expansion Conversations

Use when a client is in the "Expansion" relationship stage and has expressed
interest in additional products or usage.

- Lead with the business outcome the client already told us they care about,
  not the product feature list.
- Ask who else needs to be looped in before a purchase decision (economic
  buyer, technical reviewer, procurement).
- Surface known adoption or implementation concerns before the client raises
  them — it builds trust and gives us room to address them on our terms.
- Do not promise a specific implementation timeline without confirming
  capacity with the delivery team.
